What I'm doing right now...

My blog talks a little about some of my interests and happenings, but doesn't describe what I'm actually doing day-to-day. I aim to update this page relatively frequently to tell you what I'm currently up to in and outside of work.

Last updated: 13th February 2023

  • Finished up at Tesco Bank recently, starting as a principal software engineer at Booking.com and looking forward to using all my skills on a new challenge.
  • Working on a new (and currently secret) side project when I have a little bit of spare time.
  • Keeping on top of my weight and fresh air lately by making sure I get out for a decent walk every day; come rain, shine or forgotten house keys.
  • Still enjoying driving the Civic Type-R as the main car, although there are now a couple of cosmetic fixes it needs.
  • Waiting on the Discovery coming back from its MOT work where it should be in a good place to keep going for the rest of the year.

  • Finishing off the renovations on our first floor. We have no heating on the whole floor right now, so we're living on a mattress in my office/loft right now.
